About

My name is Linghai Liu. I am now a junior at Brown University concentrating in Applied Mathematics - Computer Science. Exploring the unknown in the world with mathematical tools always fancies me, as well as learning new topics and employing them.

Aside from school and work, I enjoy reading books and watching anime. Some of my favorites are Snow Country (雪国) by Yasunari Kawabata (川端 康成) and The End of the Affair by Graham Greene. The first demonstrates the innocence of a human heart, as it melts into a pure, white, untainted world, while the latter blatently narrates a record of hate far more than of love.
